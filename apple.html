Apple

January 24, 2022

I thought there was no greater hatred than Microsoft until I was working on a web job for a friend and discovered that for 'performance reasons' iOS browsers (not just Safari, literally every browser on iOS!) don't support background-position: fixed;. I was talking about Apple to an advocate a while back and they mentioned 'right to repair'. Well, another Apple devotee I know recently informed me that the new Macs are super cool - despite the fact that they are desktop computers in which you can't even install new RAM, much less replace a fan or add more storage. How Apple is still in business I don't know.
