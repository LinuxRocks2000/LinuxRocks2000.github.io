Linuxrocks2000 Is Back

March 6, 2022

After almost a year, the comic is back! I abandoned it when I thought nobody was actively reading it, having lost interest myself, but here it is again! I'll try to post at least once a week but I've picked up a number of commitments over this year and I don't know if I can. This reincarnation was largely spurred by the comments from new readers, some of whom I don't know in real life; which I consider to be a large hallmark in this still-infant comic. Thanks for reading!
